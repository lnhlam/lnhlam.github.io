Kippoverse

Mobile App
Platform: Android/iOS

The Kippoverse is a 2D mobile metaverse extension of the dating app known as Kippo. Originally the Dating App for Gamers, Kippo has built a social world within the app where users can meet, voice chat, and connect. All in-game assets are made in Adobe Illustrator, animated in either After Effects or Spine Esoteric, and implemented in Unity.

Role: 2D Artist/Animator
