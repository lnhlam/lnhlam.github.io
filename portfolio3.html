Ninja Chowdown

Mobile Game Application
Collaborative Project
Platform: iOS/Android

Ninja Chowdown is a pixel based mobile application featuring a ninja rushing through the streets in pursuit of the legendary Knownut. The game is an automatic side scrolling runner where the player must dodge obstacles, eat donuts, and defeat anything in their path.

Collaborators:
Programmer: Mark Ilog
Music and Sound Design: Johnson Nguyen
Publishing: Abylight Studios
