Undead Carnival Carnage

Video Game
Collaborative Project
Platform: HTML 5

Undead Carnival Carnage is a mini platform survival game made during the Ludum Dare 47 game jam, themed "Stuck in a Loop". The game features Jared, the carnival attendant, as he pushes through hordes of zombies while trapped on a panic-inducing carousel of CARNAGE!

This game was awarded 1st place in the graphics category and 17th place overall among over 3000 submissions.
