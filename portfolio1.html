5-Second Sumo

Mobile Game Application
Collaborative Study
Platform: Android
Google Play

5-Second Sumo is a mobile application where the player must throw
food off of a moving conveyer belt to feed a hungry sumo wrestler
without having food stay on the floor for longer than 5 seconds.
The game is essentially based off of the “5-second rule” where
food that hit the floor is edible as long as it is picked up within 5 seconds.

Collaborators:
Programmer: Mark Ilog
Music and Sound Design: Johnson Nguyen
