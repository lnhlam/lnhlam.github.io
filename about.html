Hi, I'm Linh.

But you can call me Lenny! I am an illustrator, animator, and motion graphic designer from Southern California!

As a kid, I was the one who drew all over my notebooks and loved all things video games and cartoons. Throughout college and my career, I've enjoyed staying curious and dabbled in graphic design, programming, motion graphics, animation, and it all ultimately led me to game development. I am always looking to grow as an artist and strive to become better at my craft, and hopefully my work speaks for itself!

Please take a look through and enjoy my work!
