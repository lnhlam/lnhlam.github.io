Underling Uprising

Video Game
Collaborative Project
Projected Platform: PC/Nintendo Switch

Underling Uprising is a 2D, arcade, saturday morning-inspired Beat 'em up game about 4 failed science experiments rising up to take down their mad scientist creator, Dr. Baldrick. This project is currently in development and was funded on Kickstarter raising over $15,000. The character bases are created in Adobe Animate, animated in Spine Esoteric, and certain attacks and actions are purely frame-by-frame animation.

Role: Art Direction, Character Artist, Animator

Collaborators:
Programmer: Mark Ilog
Music and Sound Design: Johnson Nguyen
Writing: Christopher Vasquez
Background Artists: Richard Nguyen, Brandon Busante, Dwight Ong
