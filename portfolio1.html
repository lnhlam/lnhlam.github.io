Cheez-o-Rama

Branding and Packaging
Carton: 9 x 3 x 3 in
Take-out Box: 2 x 2 x 2 in
Box: 1.5 x 3 x 3 in
Poster: 17 x 11 in
Brochure: 13 x 19 in

Cheez-o-Rama is a three part identity branding project
based on a fictional festival commemorating cheese and
other cheese products. Cheez-o-Rama includes: an eight-fold
brochure, a promotional poster, and three different types
of packaging samples.
