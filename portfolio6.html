Underling Uprising

Branding
Commission
Banner: 24 x 36 ft

Based off of the personification of a computer virus and an avatar characterized as a firewall protection system. The banner was commissioned by Cal Poly Pomona’s Stop Think Connect program and was presented on the side of the school’s parking structure to advertise the 2016 Cyber Security and Awareness Fair. The right portion of the design is six feet wide and contains the information for the year’s fair, whereas the left portion of the design is determined to be used for future Cyber Security and Awareness Fairs.
