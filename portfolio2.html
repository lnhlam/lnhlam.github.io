Turtle Daddy

Mobile Game Application
Collaborative Project
Platform: iOS/Android/HTML5

Turtle Daddy is a pixel based mobile game application featuring an enthusiastic Turtle Daddy commando who travels beaches and saves baby turtles from the dangers of the shorelines on their trek from birth to sea.

Collaborators:
Programmer: Mark Ilog
Music and Sound Design: Johnson Nguyen



*Please note that this is the jam version of the game. The fully polished version is still in progress.
